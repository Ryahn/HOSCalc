Privacy Policy

This Privacy Policy describes how your personal information is collected, used, and shared when you use HOS Calc (the "App").

Information We Collect

When you use the App, we automatically collect certain information about your device, including:

  • Device ID: A unique identifier for your device
  • Break times and durations that you input into the app
  • Usage statistics of the app features

How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect to:

  • Provide and maintain the App's functionality
  • Track and display your break history
  • Generate anonymous statistics about app usage
  • Improve the App's features and user experience

Data Storage

The App stores your break history and settings locally on your device. Some data is also stored on our secure servers to enable features like global statistics and data backup.

Data Sharing

We do not sell or share your personal information with third parties. Anonymous usage statistics may be used to improve the App's functionality.

Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access your personal information
  • Delete your data from our servers
  • Opt out of future data collection

Security

We implement appropriate security measures to protect your personal information. However, no method of transmission over the internet is 100% secure, and we cannot guarantee absolute security.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time to reflect changes in our practices or for other operational, legal, or regulatory reasons. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new privacy policy in the App.

Contact Us

For questions about this Privacy Policy, please contact us at support@hoscalc.info.
